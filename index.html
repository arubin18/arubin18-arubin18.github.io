newspark is an online donation platform that bridges the gap between viewing online content and donating to the causes we care about. With newspark, let's make the world a better place - one click at a time.

About us

Our Mission

Wildfires in California. The outbreak of Coronavirus. Issues like these arise constantly. We learn about them via news articles or social media. Online content makes us sad and frustrated. We all want to make a difference, but few of us donate to these issues. There is a gap between viewing content and taking action. When we search for charities, there are too many. When we finally find one, how do we know it is trustworthy? These obstacles prevent people from donating. Our mission is to solve this problem.
